FLOW Workshop @ IROS 2026

From Local Observations to World Models

Robots operating in human environments must navigate spaces populated by people. This workshop explores how large-scale observations of human motion can inform robot navigation through learned motion priors.

Invited Speakers

  • Henrik I. Christensen — UC San Diego
  • Ely Repiso — University of Málaga
  • Francesco Zanlungo — University of Palermo

FLOW Shared-Space Navigation Challenge

The workshop introduces a new benchmark for robot navigation in real human environments derived from large-scale human trajectory datasets.

Dataset, evaluation scripts, and baseline methods will be released here.
